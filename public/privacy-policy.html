Privacy Policy — Tesco ERM

Last updated: 19 June 2026  ·  Effective: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tesco Structures LLP (“we”, “our”, “Company”) collects, uses, and protects information through the Tesco ERM mobile application and the Tesco ERM Web portal (together, the “Service”). Tesco ERM is an internal employee resource management tool used by employees of Tesco Structures for attendance, leave, allowance, payroll, and live field-work tracking. It is not a consumer product and is distributed to employees through their company-issued credentials.

1. Who this Policy Applies To

This Policy applies to all current and past employees of Tesco Structures who use the Tesco ERM mobile or web application, and to their personal data processed through the Service. By signing in with the credentials issued by your HR team, you acknowledge this Policy.

3. Background Location — Specific Disclosure

The app collects location data in the background so HR can see live presence during your shift even when the app is not in the foreground. This continues only while you are checked in and stops automatically the moment you tap Check Out, when the device location services are turned off, or when location permission is revoked. Background tracking never runs outside an active shift.

A persistent notification is shown by the operating system while background location tracking is active, so you always know when it is running. You may revoke the “Allow all the time” permission at any moment from your device settings; doing so will stop live-tracking but you can still check in and check out manually.

4. How We Use the Information

  • To process attendance, leave, permission, allowance, and payslip workflows.
  • To calculate work hours, late-marks, half-day deductions and reimbursable distance for petrol allowance.
  • To enable your manager and HR to review, approve, or reject your requests, and to notify you of the outcome.
  • To produce internal HR reports and payroll inputs.
  • To send transactional notifications (OTP, approval status, company announcements).
  • To improve app stability through aggregated, anonymised crash and error data.
  • To comply with applicable laws, internal policies, and to respond to lawful requests.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process the data on the basis of (a) the employment relationship between you and Tesco Structures, (b) our legitimate business interest in workforce administration, (c) compliance with applicable Indian labour and tax laws, and (d) your consent for optional features such as background location tracking.

6. Sharing & Third-Party Processors

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party marketers. Personal data is accessed only by authorised HR personnel and your reporting manager within Tesco Structures.

We use the following sub-processors to operate the Service. Each is contractually bound to handle data only on our instructions:

  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting (servers in India / nearby region).
  • Render — application backend hosting.
  • Vercel / Hostinger — web frontend hosting.
  • Google Maps Platform — map rendering and geocoding for live tracking and travel allowance.
  • SendGrid / Brevo — transactional email delivery (OTP, notifications).
  • Expo Application Services — mobile build, push notification delivery, over-the-air updates.

We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, or protect the rights, property, or safety of Tesco Structures, our employees, or others.

7. Data Retention

We retain attendance, leave, allowance, and payroll records for the period required under Indian labour, tax, and audit laws (typically seven years). Location pings are retained for up to twelve months and then aggregated or deleted. Crash logs are retained for ninety days. When your employment with Tesco Structures ends, your account is deactivated; historical records are retained for the legally-required period and then deleted.

8. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS / TLS. Passwords are stored as one-way hashes. Access to production databases is restricted to named administrators. We perform regular dependency and vulnerability reviews. While no system can be guaranteed perfectly secure, we maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

9. Your Rights

You may, subject to applicable law and your employment terms:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data through your HR team.
  • Request deletion of data that is no longer required for legal or HR purposes.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as background location tracking.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, write to the contact below.

10. Children's Privacy

Tesco ERM is intended solely for Tesco Structures employees, all of whom are over 18. The Service is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app announcement and by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after a change indicates your acknowledgement of the revised Policy.
