01 Introduction & scope

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how Tesco Structures LLP (“Tesco Structures”, “we”, “our”, or “the Company”) collects, uses, retains, and safeguards personal information that is processed through the Tesco ERM mobile application for Android and the Tesco ERM Web portal (together, the “Service”).

Tesco ERM is an internal employee resource management tool. It is used by employees of Tesco Structures to mark attendance, apply for leave and permission, submit travel and petrol allowance claims, request payslips, and to enable HR to monitor live field-work status during a shift. The Service is distributed exclusively to current employees through company-issued credentials and is not intended for use by the general public.

By signing in to the Service with the credentials provided to you by the HR team, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

02 Definitions

Personal data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, including name, employee ID, email, phone, location, photos, and login identifiers.
Processing
Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, transmission, alteration, and deletion.
Sub-processor
A third-party service provider that processes personal data on our behalf under contractual data-protection terms.
Foreground / Background location
Location collected while the app is on screen (“foreground”) or while it is minimised or the screen is off (“background”).

04 Background location — specific disclosure

The Service collects location in the background so that HR can see live presence during your shift even when the app is not on screen. Background collection is strictly bounded: it starts only when you tap Check In and stops the moment you tap Check Out, when device location services are turned off, or when you revoke location permission for the app. Background tracking does not run outside an active shift, and it does not run on weekends, holidays, or leave days.

A persistent system notification is shown by the operating system whenever background location is active, so that you always know it is running. You may revoke the “Allow all the time” permission at any time from your device settings. Doing so disables live tracking but does not prevent you from checking in or out manually.

05 How we use the information

  • To process attendance, leave, permission, allowance, and payslip workflows.
  • To calculate work hours, late marks, half-day deductions, and reimbursable distance for petrol allowance.
  • To enable your manager and HR to review, approve, or reject your requests, and to notify you of the outcome.
  • To produce internal HR reports and payroll inputs.
  • To send transactional notifications such as OTP, approval status, and company announcements.
  • To monitor and improve the stability and security of the Service through aggregated, de-identified crash and error data.
  • To comply with applicable laws, internal policies, and to respond to lawful requests of competent authorities.

06 Legal basis for processing

We process personal data on the following bases: (a) the employment relationship between you and Tesco Structures; (b) our legitimate business interest in workforce administration and operational oversight; (c) compliance with applicable Indian labour, tax, and statutory record-keeping laws; and (d) your explicit consent for optional features such as background location tracking, granted through your device permission prompts.

08 Data retention

We retain attendance, leave, allowance, and payroll records for the period required under Indian labour, tax, and audit laws, which is typically seven (7) years from the end of the relevant financial year. Location pings are retained for up to twelve (12) months and are then either aggregated for statistical reporting or permanently deleted. Crash logs and diagnostic data are retained for ninety (90) days.

When your employment with Tesco Structures ends, your account is deactivated immediately. Historical records are retained for the legally required period and are then deleted in accordance with our internal retention schedule.

09 Security

All data in transit between your device and our servers is protected by industry-standard TLS encryption. Passwords are stored only as one-way salted hashes; we never store passwords in plain text. Access to the production database is restricted to a small number of named administrators and is logged. We perform regular dependency reviews and apply security patches promptly. While no system can be guaranteed completely secure, we maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards that are appropriate to the sensitivity of the data.

10 Your rights

Subject to applicable law and your employment terms, you may:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or out-of-date data through your HR contact.
  • Request deletion of data that is no longer required for legal or HR purposes.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as background location tracking, at any time, through your device settings.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant Indian data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please write to the contact provided in section 13.

11 Children's privacy

Tesco ERM is intended solely for employees of Tesco Structures, all of whom are aged 18 years or older. The Service is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it without undue delay.

12 Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Service or in applicable law. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app announcement and by updating the “Effective from” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service after a change indicates your acknowledgement of the revised Policy.
